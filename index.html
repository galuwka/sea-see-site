About us

"Sea see you" is your online source for ethically-produced, eco-friendly lingerie, swimwear, underwear and accessories, all handmade by the sea in Kyiv, Ukraine. The recognition we receive for our fun, simple, striking designs is as important to us as our commitment to the environment: we use recycled, re-used and reclaimed fabrics, minimise waste, and work with line ends and local suppliers wherever possible to control our carbon footprint. We use only biodegradable and recyclable packaging and are always working on new ways to make our studio more green. We pride ourselves in the care in our processes, from hand-drafting patterns and carefully selecting fabrics, to hand finishing each garment, before dispatching to our valued and loyal customers.
